HYPNOTHERAPY

I have been a Certified Hypnotherapist since 2016, and am thrilled to present this life affirming freedom "technology" to you. Hypnosis is essentially a very deeply relaxed state where we communicate directly with the unconscious mind to assist with making positive transformations.

As we move through life, over time we sometimes notice we have thought patterns or behaviors that are holding us back and not helping us to get what we want out of life. These thoughts, behaviors, actions, and feelings that are not in alignment with our true selves are controlled by our unconscious mind. They are programs: just like our computers have operating systems, so do we! We also have the ability to upgrade the programs we are running on - that is what hypnotherapy does!

Would you like to learn more about hypnosis and how it can help you to be more comfortable, free, happy, and lighter in your life, and help to achieve a more balanced and creative state of mind in your daily activities? Contact me today!
