Researcher and Stanford Professor Emeritus Dr. William Tiller says that when information carried through the Reconnective Healing frequencies is introduced, it creates coherence, order, and a greater harmony and balance within us.

What can I expect during a Reconnective Healing session? What’s the best way to prepare for it? What can I do to allow it to be most effective?

Everyone’s experience with Reconnective Healing is unique. Often healings are reported in an instant in just one session. Sometimes it takes a bit longer for a healing to unfold. As a general rule, the less you expect, the more you receive. Just wear comfortable clothes and socks as you’ll be removing your shoes.

Is Reconnective Healing like Reiki or Qigong?

Reiki and Qigong, just like other energy healing modalities, access various different subsets or portions of the energy field. Reconnective Healing accesses the entirety of the energy field, and brings you beyond into newer aspects of light and information. What does this mean to you? It means that the Reconnective Healing frequencies bring you all the gifts of all our known energy healing techniques and then brings you far beyond them. And this is a truly wonderful gift!
